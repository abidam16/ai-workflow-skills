# Section Guide

## Product Overview
What the product/change is, who it serves, and why it exists.

## Product Goals
What the product is trying to achieve now.

## Product Non-Goals
What is intentionally out of scope now.

## Users, Roles, and Actors
Who participates in the system and what authority or responsibility they have.

## Core Domains
Main business/product areas.

## Core User Flows
Most important end-to-end flows.

## Product Rules
Rules that must remain true regardless of implementation.

## Current Behavior by Domain
How the system behaves today, including temporary or legacy behavior.

## Target Behavior / Desired Product Direction
What should become true relative to current behavior.

## Known Product Problems
Important gaps, inconsistencies, friction, or risks.

## Success Criteria
What makes the change successful from a product/business perspective.

## Open Product Questions
Important unresolved product questions. Do not guess.

## Deferred Product Directions
Future directions intentionally postponed.
