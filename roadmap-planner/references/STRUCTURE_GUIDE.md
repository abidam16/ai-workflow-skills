# Structure Guide

## Product Roadmap Structure
1. Overview
2. Strategic Delivery Logic
3. Active Initiatives
4. Deferred / Later Initiatives
5. Completed / Established Capabilities (optional)
6. Open Strategic Questions
7. Immediate Next Step

## Initiative Roadmap Structure
1. Initiative Overview
2. Delivery Strategy
3. Phase 0: Foundation (optional)
4. Phase 1: MVP
5. Phase 2+: Enhancement phases as needed
6. Cross-Cutting Concerns
7. Deferred / Later
8. Open Delivery Questions
9. Plan Handoff
10. Immediate Next Step
