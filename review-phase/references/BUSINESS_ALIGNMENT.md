# Business Alignment Review

Business alignment is mandatory. A review is incomplete if it checks only technical correctness.

Check these questions explicitly:

1. Does the implementation address the stated business need or product problem?
2. Does it support the intended user or operator workflow?
3. Are the important product / business rules preserved?
4. Does it satisfy the approved goals and stay within the non-goals?
5. Does it materially improve the intended outcome, or only appear complete technically?
6. Did the implementation introduce workflow friction, confusion, or operational burden that undermines the business goal?
7. Are success criteria meaningfully supported, partially supported, or not supported?

## Rating guidance

For business alignment, state one of:

- `FULFILLED`
- `PARTIALLY_FULFILLED`
- `NOT_FULFILLED`
- `DEVIATED`

Explain the reason briefly and concretely.
